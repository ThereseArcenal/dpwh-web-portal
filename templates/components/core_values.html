Core Values

The guiding principles that shape every project, every decision, and every public servant in the Department of Public Works and Highways.

PUBLIC SERVICE

Serving the Filipino people with dedication and responsiveness to their infrastructure needs.

PROFESSIONALISM

Excellence and continuous improvement in engineering and project management.

TEAMWORK

Collaborating across all bureaus and regions to achieve organizational goals.

INTEGRITY

Striving for the highest quality in all infrastructure projects and services.
