Our Foundation

Core Values

The guiding principles that shape every project, every decision, and every public servant in the Department of Public Works and Highways.

MANDATE

The planning, design, construction and maintenance of infrastructure facilities especially national highways, flood control and water resource development systems, and other public works in accordance with national development objectives.

PUBLIC SERVICE

Anticipating and responding to the needs of all stakeholders and satisfying their needs through quality effort, DPWH officials and employees shall the deliver the kind of facilities and services that are responsive to the needs of the Filipino people.

INTEGRITY

Conducting one's business is transparent honest and ethical manner, DPWH officials and employees shall maximize the effectiveness of the public's resources entrusted to them practice simple living and have the moral courage to make tough decisions and to do what is right for the public interest. They shall be accountable to the public and to their peers for their actions and decisions and to do that is right to the nation. They shall do the right things the right way.

PROFESSIONALISM

Taking pride in one's work and comitted to the pursuit of excellence. DPWH officials and employees shall act completely, ehtically, dilligently and with accountability and dignity in their areas of expertise. They shall be given challenging assignments and training and promotions shall be based on merit.

EXCELLENCE

By continuously pursuing the highest quality of work in serving the public, DPWH officials and employees shall deliver the highests quality results beyond what is expected and required of them one time, just-in-time and all the time.

TEAMWORK

Working together in an environment of trust, fairness, respect, cooperation and communication, DPWH officials and employees shall focus their efforts in the attainment of the vision, mission, and goals of the Department.
