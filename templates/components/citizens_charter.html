Citizens's Charter

The Citizens's Charter is a document that outlines our commitment to providing excellent service to the people. It establishes clear standards and expectations for all our employees.

We pledge to deliver transparent, efficient, and responsive public service to all citizens of the Philippines. Our charter guides our actions and ensures accountability at every level of our organization.

Key Principles

  • Transparency in all operations
  • Efficient service delivery
  • Accountability to stakeholders
  • Continuous improvement
  • Citizen-centered approach
