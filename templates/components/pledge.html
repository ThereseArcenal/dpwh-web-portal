DPWH Quality Policy

The Department of Public Works and Highways (DPWH) is committed to delivering quality infrastructure projects that meet established standards of safety, efficiency, and sustainability. The department ensures compliance with all applicable laws and continuously improves its Quality Management System to provide reliable public service and national development.

Basically, their goal is to build infrastructure that is:

  • • Safe
  • • Durable
  • • Cost-Effective
  • • On Time
  • • Beneficial to the public
